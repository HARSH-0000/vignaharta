Vighna Harta

Lord Ganesha is revered as the remover of obstacles and the patron of arts and sciences. He is invoked at the beginning of any new venture or important work.

Significance of Ganesh Utsav

Ganesh Utsav is a 11-day Hindu festival celebrating the birth of Lord Ganesha. The festival begins on Ganesh Chaturthi and ends on Anant Chaturdashi. During this period, elaborate pandals are set up, prayers are offered, and the community comes together in celebration. The festival culminates with the immersion of Ganesha idols in water bodies, symbolizing the cycle of creation and dissolution.

11 Days of Celebration

Day 1 - Ganesh Chaturthi

Installation of Ganesha idol with Pranapratishtha ceremony

Day 2-10 - Daily Worship

Morning and evening aarti, offerings of modak, and community prayers

Day 11 - Anant Chaturdashi

Grand procession and immersion ceremony (Visarjan)
